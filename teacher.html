About

The Paragon Academy strives to be an ideal institution where aspirants from different strata gather to make their dream come true by becoming the part of bureaucracy. The Paragon aims to provide quality guidance and conducive environment to students enabling them to harness their true potential to excel in the most superior examination of the country. The academy believes to offer level playing field to rich and poor alike by discouraging commercialism in the education sector. It is an endeavor to bring change in society by producing young bureaucrats that may accelerate the progress of our beloved country.
